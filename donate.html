Support Pehiāweri Ahuwhenua Trust

Kia ora whānau. Thank you for considering a donation to the Pehiāweri Ahuwhenua Trust. Your contribution directly supports the ongoing maintenance of this website and the development process of our whenua.

There are two ways you can donate:

💳

Donate Online

Quick and easy — make a secure payment online using Stripe, our trusted payment provider.

Note on fees: Stripe deducts a small processing fee from each donation. For example, on a $1 donation, Stripe deducts $0.329 (2.9% + NZ$0.30). The remaining $0.671 goes directly to the Trust.
Donate Now via Stripe
🏦

Direct Bank Transfer

Make a donation directly from your online banking system to the Trust's bank account. This means 100% of your donation reaches the Trust — no processing fees deducted.
