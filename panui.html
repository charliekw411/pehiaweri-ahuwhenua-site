Updates, strategic plans and important resources for the Pehiāweri B1B community.

Pehiāweri B1B Strategic Plan 2017–2021

Inside, you will find a comprehensive outline of our goals, strategies, and initiatives aimed at the sustainable development and management of the Pehiāweri B1B block. The document includes detailed action plans, timelines, and responsibilities, ensuring alignment with the cultural values and aspirations of the Ngāti Hau uri.

Additionally, it highlights key milestones, resource allocation, and performance indicators to guide our progress.

Follow Us on Facebook

You can follow the Pehiāweri B1B kaupapa on Facebook, where we are more regularly accessible and engaged with our community. On our page, you'll find updates on ongoing projects, community events, and important announcements.

We also share cultural insights, photos, and videos that highlight the activities and achievements of the Ngāti Hau uri. Join us to stay connected and informed about our latest developments and initiatives.

Māori Land Court

Pehiāweri B1B Shareholder Details

The following link will guide you to whether you are a shareholder and give you the details of the landblock.

  1. Go to www.maorilandcourt.govt.nz
  2. Go to Māori Land Online (bottom left)
  3. Click Block Search → add Pehiāweri B1B → click Search
  4. If land is still under a deceased whānau name, apply through Māori Land Court
  5. Click Make an Application → Succession to Māori Land
